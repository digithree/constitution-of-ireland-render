For Participants

The show lasts for about 50 minutes and is for one participant at a time. You must take this journey on your own.

A new participant will start their journey every 15 minutes, starting at 2.30pm each day.

So the first starts at 2.30pm, the second at 2.45pm, the third at 3pm, etc.
